My Approach

.

My approach is to redefine the process of searching, purchasing, and viewing products with a better navigation system to help customers to find the right products. Another approach is to build a system for admin to manage their products and customers' accounts and their orders.

Log in/ Sign up feature.

This is a page for registered users to log in, with a form for new users to sign up. Users can shop online and write a review.

Customer who are not signed up with us.

Customers who do not have an account with us can only view products, image galleries, and all the reviews. They can sign up for an account with us for free.

Log in as customers.

Registered customers can view all the products, image galleries, and reviews. They can also add reviews and delete their own review. They can edit their account information including changing their passwords. They can shop online, add as well as remove products from the shopping cart. Payment processing is implemented by PayPal API. Once payments go through, customers will see a thank you page.

Log in as an admin.

Admin can add, view, update, and delete a product, and image gallery. Admin can update, delete, and approve reviews that are submitted by customers, as well as see customers' orders. Admin also can view, add, update, and delete all the customers' accounts, but they cannot access customers' passwords.
