My Approach

.

My approach is to redefine the process of searching, purchasing, and viewing products with a better navigation system to help customers to find the right products. Another approach is to build a system for admin to manage their products and customers' accounts and their orders.

Admin

People who manage the website

Primary Users

A registered customer

Secondary Users

Customer who are not signed up with us

Log in/ Sign up feature.

A page for registered users to log in and a form for new users to sign up. New users can sign up as a customer and can shopping online, write a review.

Customer who are not signed up with us.

Customers who have not an account with us, they can only view products, image gallery, and all the reviews. They can sign up for an account with us.

Log in as customers.

A registered customer can view all the products, image galleries, reviews. They can also add a review and delete their own review. They can edit their account information including changing their passwords. They can shop online, add products into the shopping cart as well as delete a product. They can pay through PayPal. If payments go through, it will go to a thank you page. Payment Processing will be implemented by PayPal API.

Log in as an admin.

Admin can add, view, update, and delete a product. Admin can add, delete, update, view products' image gallery. Admin can update, delete, and approve a review that is submitted by customers. Admin also can view, add, update, delete all the customers' accounts. But they cannot access customers' passwords. Admin can see customers' orders.
